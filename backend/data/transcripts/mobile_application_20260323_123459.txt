Q1: What inspired you to start developing a mobile application, and what specific problem or need were you aiming to address with it?

A1: I started building the app because I kept seeing people juggle too many disconnected tools, and I wanted to create something that quietly simplified their day without adding more noise. The goal was to solve that everyday friction by giving users a single, intelligent place where their tasks, context, and next steps naturally came together.

Q2: Can you describe how your app helps users integrate their tasks, context, and next steps into one cohesive experience?

A2: It brings tasks, calendar events, and contextual cues into a single timeline that updates dynamically based on what the user is doing and where they are. By blending that context with lightweight AI, the app surfaces the next logical step at just the right moment, so the whole experience feels unified and effortless rather than like juggling separate tools.

Q3: How did you decide on the specific features and technologies to implement in your app to ensure that it effectively integrates tasks, context, and next steps without overwhelming the user?

A3: I focused on just a few high‑impact features and used lightweight, unobtrusive AI so the app felt supportive rather than busy. I also validated every addition with quick user tests, keeping only the elements that genuinely reduced friction and made the experience feel more seamless.
