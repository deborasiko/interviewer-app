FULL TRANSCRIPT

Q1: How do you balance staying true to your personal work style while adapting to the varying needs and expectations of different teams or projects you've worked on?

A1: I stay true to my style by keeping my core habits—clarity, structure, and proactive communication—consistent, while adjusting the level of detail, pace, and collaboration based on what each team responds to best. That balance lets me remain authentic while still giving every group what they need to work smoothly and confidently.

Q2: Could you share a specific example of a time when maintaining clarity and proactive communication significantly improved a team's dynamic or project outcome, and how you adapted your approach to foster that success?

A2: In a cross‑functional project, two teams kept interpreting the same requirements differently, so I started sending short alignment summaries after every discussion to make sure everyone had the same understanding. That simple habit eliminated confusion almost immediately, and by adjusting the tone and level of detail for each group, the collaboration became smoother and the project finished ahead of schedule.
